General Rule Info

All of the following rules apply to ANY AND ALL official ComparatorCraftSMP Minecraft Servers, Discord Servers, communication platforms, websites, forums, and comments to any of our social media. Any staff reserve the right to punish you on any and all ComparatorCraftSMP properties if you violate any of our guidelines. This also applies to any attempts, successful or otherwise, to exploit any loopholes you may find. Staff also have the right to change, add, or remove any rules if they deem it necessary.

If you leave the Discord server during a punishment (i.e. tempban or mute) you will be banned.

Age restriction

We do have a 15+ age restriction. We don't mind taking in 14-years who show a lot of talent, skill and passion for the game but generally we're 15+.

Warning Structure

You are entitled up to 3 warnings from staff before you will be muted on the next transgression. Violate our rules again, and you will have a 1 week mute. A further violation will warrant a temporary ban from the Minecraft server in addition to a mute on our Discord server.

Any and all staff can punish, ban, mute, kick, and warn players if they have evidence that a rule was violated on the server. All evidence will be presented to the punished party.

Chat

  1. Don't be that person. — We have no place on our server for hate speech.
    (Racism, Sexism, Homophobia, Xenophobia, Transphobia, etc.)
  2. Respect all cultures. — We have people from all around the world. Please avoid topics based on politics or religion.
  3. It's not always banter. — Use common sense. Try not to intentionally trigger, annoy, or hurt someone. It might be harmless to you but to some people it can be hurtful. Think before you speak!
  4. SFW only. — Please don't send porn, gore, or overly edgy memes and videos. Also, don't talk about gross, gory, or lewd activities. We don't mind shitposting but don't take it too far.
  5. Don't spam. — This is straightforward. Avoid filling any chat with repeated messages or similar messages.

Griefing/Stealing

  1. Griefing. — If mine blocks, ignite TNT, kill mobs, place any new blocks without consent, or are the direct cause of destruction in a community build or players build: That counts as griefing.
    If you accidentally cause destruction on a aplayers base or build: Contact the player or staff immediately.
  2. No player killing/trapping without consent. — Killing a player without their consent for PvP is not OK. If you're the cause of another player's death that counts as PvP.
  3. Stealing. — This applies to looting chests AND taking placed blocks.
    We monitor player's interactions with chests heavily. You can't get away with stealing.

Exploits

  1. Exploits. — Anything that gives an unfair advantage should not be usesd and if discovered should be reported to staff immediately. See exceptions below.
    (If you have discovered an exploit, please open a report/support ticket.)
    This includes exploits in server software, permissions in game and on discord, plugin, and datapack exploits. Not to mention exploits in Minecraft itself.
  2. Seed X-Ray / Math Mining. — No seed-based x-raying or math mining in any way. (Don't use seed generation and seed exploits to find diamonds.)
  3. Lag Machines / Intentional Lag. — Do not intentionally degrade server performance with machines, excessive entities, redstone, or otherwise. All redstone contraptions should be togglable or default to the off position when possible.

    Allowed Exceptions

    Also if your farm is laggy: Staff reserve the right to ask you to turn certain machines off. If you're afking and the server is lagging: Staff also reserve the right to kick/tempban you from the server.
  4. Duping. — Duping items through any method is not permitted except in the exceptions outlined below.
    • Bedrock Breaking
    • End Portal/Portap Frame Removal (not including the communal portal or main island gateways without prior approval from staff)
    • Update Suppression Machines (please only use when the server is quiet to respect your fellow players
    • TNT Duping
    • Carpet Duping
    • Rail Duping

Hacking/Banned Mods

  1. Hacked Clients — Not allowed for any reason. 3rd party clients such as Lunar Client are OK provided features do not give a distinct gameplay advantage. If in doubt, ask in #support.
  2. Banned Mods — Mods such as X-Ray or Entity Tracking and more are not allowed. You can view a full list of banned mods below. If in doubt, ask staff.
    This includes Baraitone and x-ray texture packs.

Advertising

  1. Advertising — Don't send any server IPs, Discord server invites, or website/forum links to any other Minecraft server without staff approval. This also includes mentioning a separate Minecraft server's name.
    An exception to this rule is insanely popular servers like Hypixel, Hive, CubeCraft, etc. As well as YouTuber SMPs, SMPs that don't actively recruit, or SMPs that don't send out whitelist applications like HermitCraft, SciCraft, TIS Trinity, DreamSMP, ProtoTech, etc.

Illegal Content/Piracy

  1. Illegal Content/Piracy — Do not share, mention, or teach/show methods of illegally obtaining content. This includes sharing links, sharing tutorials, or just mentioning applications used to obtain illegal content.
    We're making this a rule because it is against Discord's TOS.

Voice Chat

  1. Respect — Please be respectful when you're in a VC, everything in rule 1 also applies when you're in voice chat.
  2. Volume — Please avoid intentionally changing your microphone settings to make it super loud and obnoxious.
  3. Spam — Don't spam soundboards or intentionally play audio that is annoying and disturbs people.
  4. Trolling — Don't join calls, say/do something obnoxious, then instantly leave the call.
